Revamp and Upgrade Mobile Application

In this project, our primary goal is to revamp and upgrade our existing mobile application to create a more engaging and seamless experience for our customers. The current application has served its purpose well but now requires a refresh to stay competitive in the market and meet the evolving needs of our users.

User Interface Redesign: One of the key areas of focus will be the redesign of the user interface (UI) to provide a more intuitive and visually appealing experience for our customers. This will involve improving navigation, enhancing visual elements, and ensuring consistency across all screens.

Feature Enhancements: We aim to introduce new features and functionalities to the app to add value for our customers. This may include but is not limited to, a personalized recommendation engine, in-app messaging for customer support, integration with social media platforms, and improved search capabilities.

Performance Optimization: It is crucial for the app to run smoothly and efficiently to keep users engaged. Our Agile Development Process

We delicately follow the Agile + iterative approach to work on each of our projects. We ensure the deliverables remain seamless, flawless and are completed within the set time frame.

Our agile methodology aims to deliver the right product with incremental and frequent delivery of small chunks of functionality through small cross-functional self-organizing teams, enabling frequent customer feedback and course correction as needed. Requirements, plans, and results are evaluated continuously, so our agile teams have a natural mechanism for responding to change quickly.

Evaluation + Measurements of Success

To ensure transparency and keep you informed, the following practices will be implemented throughout the development period as below. By implementing these practices, we aim to foster transparency, keep you informed, and provide a visual representation of the project's development progress.

01
Daily Status Update

At the end of each day, a comprehensive email will be sent summarizing the completed work and ongoing tasks. This will provide a clear overview of the progress made.

02
Progressive Builds

Scheduled intervals will be established for sending progressive builds tailored to the respective platforms. These builds will showcase the evolving development and enable you to track the project's advancement.

03
Task Limitations and Alternatives

In the event that backend tasks or other factors hinder the generation of builds, screenshots or videos will be shared as relevant alternatives.
